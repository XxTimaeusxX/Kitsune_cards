KitsuneCards
Install Instructions
1.Android:
Download the provided APK file.
Transfer it to your Android device.
On your device, enable Install from Unknown Sources in settings.
Open the APK file to install the game.
Launch the game from your app drawer.

2.Unity Build exe:
go inside the Kitsune Cards folder.
click on the Exe and game will load.

Controls
•Drag & Drop:
Drag cards from your hand onto the white UI panel to play them.

•Draw Button:
Tap/click to draw cards (if available).

•End Turn Button:
Tap/click to end your turn and let the enemy act.

•Replay Button:
Appears after a win or loss; tap/click to restart the level.

Features Present 
Turn-based card battle system (Player vs. Enemy).
Mana system: Cards require mana to play.
Multiple card types and abilities (Damage, Buff, Debuff, Block).
Visual UI indicators for turn, mana, and health.
Drag-and-drop card play (touch and mouse compatible).
Win/Lose detection and end-of-level panel.
Replay functionality.
